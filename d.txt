A planning application to make you plan the events using notes. You can add the color notes and that helped us for you, and sort out those notes on a white board and pin & stick them.
	
Features:
•	Notes types of color notes.
•	Choose color form the side bar.
•	Helper Notes.
•	Delete the Notes.
•	Edit the notes.
•	Pin & Stick them on white board.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing)
